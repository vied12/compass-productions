A man transitioning into a woman discovers she is the father to a 13 year old boy.

SID JAVED is an upstanding well respected professional who is also carrying a painful secret. Sid is transgender;
a closeted transsexual who has secretly cross-dressed all her life.  At 33, she decides to come out and transition into a woman.  When a young stranger shows up at her door claiming to be her son,  Sid is forced to confront the truth about herself and who she really is.

VENUS is a gender bending drama about the true meaning of love, 
when othersʼ expectations of who you are clash with who you want to be.

100 minutes, colour in development

Written and directed by Eisha Marjara
Produced by Joe Balass
